How to Export

  1. Open Wallet on your iPhone and tap Apple Card.
  2. Tap the card image, then tap Statements.
  3. Tap a statement month, then scroll down and tap Export Transactions.
  4. Choose CSV and AirDrop or email it to yourself.
  5. You can export multiple months — duplicates are skipped automatically.
